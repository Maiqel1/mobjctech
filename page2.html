ABOUT US

Justcall Technologies is a company based in Ilorin, Kwara state, that serves IT solutions, data management systems, and technical support to businesses in the state and outside the state. It was established in 2007 and houses prime IT experts dedicated to providing first-rate services to promote entrepreneurial progress. Recently, it opened another office in Ibadan, Oyo state, and aims to expand its reach to more states in the near future.

JustCall Technologies was registered in June 2013. We are a group of young Engineers who are IT savvy and were dissatisfied with the poor ICT services being rendered to us by existing ICT firms and we are decided to bring together our skills and do it better. Our main office is located at no. 16 harmony crescent, Tanke Bubu, off UNILORIN road, Ilorin. Our team is made up of very qualified personnel with both local and international engineering and project management experience.
